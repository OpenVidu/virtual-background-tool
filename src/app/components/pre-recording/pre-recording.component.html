We will ask you to record several videos with the typical positions when you participate in a video conference. As automatic background detection is a really difficult problem to be executed in realtime we want to make the job for the algorithm a bit easier.
Camera
Please fix the camera on a table or on top of a desktop monitor.
Don’t record the videos using a mobile phone or laptop on your hands or legs

It is important to have a still background without any change in every video
For example, you can record yourself in front of a wall, bookcase, fourniture, etc.
Please check that your shadow is not visible on the wall as it can be considered as a “movement”
Please avoid chairs to be visible while recording the video.
it is harder for the algorithm to differentiate between shoulders and chair

Please wear clothing that differs from the background
Clothing of the same color as the background can confuse the algorithm.

As a lot of people use big headphones when participating in video conferences we will try to teach the algorithm how to recognize it
If you have headphones please prepare them as we will ask you to record some videos with them.
